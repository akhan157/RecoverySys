FEAT_02 · FLIGHT

RK4 engine.
±2–3% apogee.

4th-order Runge-Kutta ascent integration at 20ms timesteps with Mach-dependent drag and Tsiolkovsky mass depletion. Full ISA atmosphere. Multi-parameter Monte Carlo dispersion with 500 iterations.

INTEGRATORRK4 · 20 ms
ATMOSPHERICISA · density + speed of sound
APOGEE_ERR±2–3% (curve) · ±5–8% (scalar)
FEAT_03 · MOTORS

RASP .eng import.
Full thrust curve.

Drop in an .eng file from OpenMotor or ThrustCurve.org. The full time-varying thrust curve drives the simulation instead of an averaged impulse. Works for custom HPR motors that aren't in any public database.

FORMATRASP .eng (OpenMotor / ThrustCurve)
CURVE_SIZE~2–5 KB typical
INTEGRATIONLINEAR @ 50ms
FEAT_04 · DISPERSION

Monte Carlo.
Wind shear model.

500-iteration multi-parameter dispersion scatter with a 95% confidence ellipse, rendered on Leaflet topographic tiles. Wind shear modeled per altitude band. Helps you pick a launch direction before you cut the heavy shock cord.

FEAT_05 · RULES

Rules engine.
Flags before launch.

Checks packing volume, drogue-without-main, airframe-to-component mass ratios, and shock cord length rules of thumb. Surfaces warnings inline as you build. Catches obvious mistakes before you drive to the range.

RULESPACKING · DEPLOY · MASS · CORD
