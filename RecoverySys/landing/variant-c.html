Recovery bay configuration. Flight simulation. Landing dispersion analysis.

An open-source browser tool for L1, L2, and L3 high-power rocketry builders. Lays out a recovery bay from a parts catalog, predicts apogee with an ISA atmospheric model, and estimates landing dispersion under wind. Runs entirely in your browser. No backend, no account, no telemetry.

500-iteration multi-parameter Monte Carlo dispersion with wind shear. Perturbs wind, Cd, mass, deploy altitude, and motor impulse. 95% confidence ellipse on Leaflet tiles.
